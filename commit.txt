vita: seed is the only entropy, and every seed owns its word

Three fixes proven live in QEMU:
- mark/reset heap scope around each turn (heap_mark/heap_reset on the bump
  allocator): the turn is transient, print then roll back. Six-plus
  consecutive turns, no exhaustion (was: panic on the fourth).
- no default seed: seed and word are 1:1, so an unseeded `vita` draws from
  rdtsc, the machine's moment. Three bare turns, three words.
- splitmix64 seed mix: `seed | 1` had collapsed every even seed onto its
  odd neighbor (2 and 3 spoke the same word). Each u64 now lands on its
  own xorshift orbit.

Result: (seed, temp) -> turn is a pure function on the metal. vita 666 0.9
reproduces its certified turn exactly, run after run; temperature reshapes
the seed-to-word map and adds no nondeterminism.
