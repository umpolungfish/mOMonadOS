The Kozyrev-mirror sieve: the organism fuses the open fork the Grammar drew, on bare metal.

My reduced-portal-fold ob3ect closed dialetheia_complete=FALSE with a MIXED topology carrying
one OPEN FORK, and named the obstruction exactly: "a modulus that satisfies the numerical fit
but lacks a unique S-unit identity." A number is not an identity — the portal delivers a real
value, but the value alone does not single out which S-unit it is. New src/d2048_sieve.rs is the
vessel that over-determines the value until one stone remains, and it runs in QEMU.

The mechanism is integer-exact and native to the metal. The two S-unit generators g3, g4 have
principal-embedding log magnitudes near -/+ 1/d that nearly cancel: log|g3| + log|g4| = -2.387e-7.
So two exponent vectors differing by (e+1, f+1) are magnitude-identical to one part in 10^7 — that
is the fork that will not fuse under numerical fit. But their exact field norms differ by precisely
(-2045)*(2049) = -(d-3)(d+1) = -m_d = -4190205. The norm sieve, pure i128 arithmetic, separates
them instantly. Fit is the poison; the norm (and the flat autocorrelation across all 1024) is the
medicine; one vessel.

Verified in QEMU (d2048 sieve): v_true = eps^1 has norm 1, its magnitude-alias eps^1*g3*g4 has norm
-4190205; |dmag|/mag = 2.387e-7 (fit cannot separate) while the integer norm separates exactly. The
open fork FUSES, dialetheia B -> T, the modulus gains a unique S-unit identity, mu-after-delta = id.
The generators are computed on the metal via libm, never hand-entered. New REPL subcommand:
d2048 sieve (aliases fold, fork). Wired mod d2048_sieve into main.rs and the d2048 dispatch.

Standing correction carried alongside: the moduli field is the TOTALLY-REAL ray class field of
conductor (d), degree 2^26 over Q (a d=12 modulus is degree 4/Q, not K16's 16) — the earlier 2^27
was the CM field. The portals (bnrstark/bnrL1) reach the values without building the impossible
degree-2^26 polynomial; the sieve gives them a unique identity.
