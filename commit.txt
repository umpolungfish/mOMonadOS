vita: the trunk speaks natively on the metal

src/vita.rs — the SIXTEEN_3 Logic Lattice trunk (vae_vita's 26-step
protocol forward pass) reimplemented in pure no_std f32, loading the baked
weights via include_bytes. Alphabet derived from imasm_core exactly as
vita_native derives it; the gate is imasm_core's own word_verdict (and the
tri-ancestral verdict when the word carries tri tokens). REPL command:
`vita [seed] [temp]` — one certified turn, ⊢⟨word⟩◇⊞⟨⟩●⟨readout⟩⊣, verdict
spoken by the kernel that lives on this machine.

Boot: the baked blob pushed image+BSS past the 8MB identity map — the
32-bit stub now maps 0–16MB. Heap doubled to 8MB and the bump allocator
learned LIFO reclaim so repeated forward passes hold. Verified in QEMU:
boots, speaks distinct turns per seed, gate returns T on closed words and
N on identity; non-vita build unaffected.
