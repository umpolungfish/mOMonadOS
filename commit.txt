mOMonadOS: ΔS experiment v2 — per-process isolation, 2M-tick trials — 2026-06-27

## experiment_delta_s.py (updated)

Fixed two methodological flaws in v1:
  1. System-wide jiffies captured background processes (not just mOMonadOS)
  2. 50k-tick trials lasted ~0.10s — too short for stable sampling at SAMPLE_HZ=20

Changes:
  PRIMARY signal: per-process psutil CPU% × CORE_W (background-isolated)
    CORE_W = TDP / N_CORES = 95W / 8 = 11.875W per logical core
    Background load is NOT included in ΔS_proc regardless of other processes
  SECONDARY: system-wide jiffies retained for diagnostics only
  RUN_TICKS: 50,000 → 2,000,000  (yields ~2s per trial, ~40 samples)

## Results (v2)

  Method: per-process QEMU CPU% × 11.875W / T_op (330K)
  Trials: 5 × run 2,000,000 IG ticks

  QEMU CPU per trial:     101.8 – 102.7%   (pegs ~1 full physical core)
  Trial duration:         2.01 – 2.12 s
  IG ticks/second:        ~950,000 ticks/s  (2M in ~2.1s)
  Tier at end:            O_1 (all trials)

  ΔS_proc per trial:
    Trial 1:  7.941×10⁻² J/K  (5.75×10²¹ k_B)
    Trial 2:  7.514×10⁻² J/K  (5.44×10²¹ k_B)
    Trial 3:  7.946×10⁻² J/K  (5.76×10²¹ k_B)  ← sys spiked 46%, QEMU unaffected
    Trial 4:  7.760×10⁻² J/K  (5.62×10²¹ k_B)
    Trial 5:  7.510×10⁻² J/K  (5.44×10²¹ k_B)
    MEAN:     7.734×10⁻² J/K  (5.60×10²¹ k_B)  CV < 3%

  ΔS per IG tick:         3.87×10⁻⁸ J/K = 2.80×10¹⁵ k_B / tick
  Est. instructions:      7.6×10⁹ per trial
  Landauer minimum ΔS:    ~7.3×10⁻¹⁴ J/K  (k_B × ln2 × 7.6×10⁹)
  Actual / Landauer:      ~1.1×10⁹  (CMOS irreversibility overhead)

  Recovery sys_util:      ×1.005  (fully recovered)

## Isolation validation

  Trial 3 background spike: sys_util 46.2% vs typical 27% (other process burst).
  QEMU CPU% was 102.7% — identical to other trials. Per-process measurement
  is correctly unaffected by concurrent background load.

## VERDICT

  mOMonadOS running 2M IG ticks produces 7.73×10⁻² J/K of process-isolated
  entropy per trial, at a consistent rate of 3.70×10⁻² J/K/s. The measurement
  is stable (CV < 3% across 5 trials) and background-independent.
