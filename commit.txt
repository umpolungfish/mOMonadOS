mOMonadOS links imasm_core: the kernel's Token is THE Token

tokens.rs no longer defines its own 12-opcode enum; Token and Family come
from imasm_core (no_std, alloc-only, same repr and variant order), the same
definition ask_native and vae_vita already derive from. Program, the
canonical loops, ROTAT, and everything downstream are untouched — they now
compose over the shared type. Variant names follow the core's casing.
Bootimage builds clean; the last hand-copied face in the constellation is
gone, and the kernel gains the glyph face (Token::code) for free.
